About

In loving memory of Grandma Irene. The most amazing, hard working and strongest woman I've ever met. Anyone that went to West Side Coin Laundry in San Benito, TX would know this woman and she probably knew you. She has touched everyone's hearts and had a business that everyone knew her by.

She helped me with whatever I needed. She practically ran the skate shop I had and maintained her laundry mat at the same time. We all miss you so much. May you rest in peace.
